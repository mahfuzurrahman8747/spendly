Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Spendly is built around a simple idea: your financial data is yours. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use Spendly, how we use it, where it is stored, and the choices you have. We have written it in plain language because privacy policies should not require a lawyer to read.

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect the information you provide directly: your name, email address, and a password (stored as a secure hash, never in plain text).

To provide the core service, we also collect the expense records you choose to log: amounts, categories, dates, and any optional notes or descriptions you add. You can edit or delete these records at any time from your account.

We also automatically collect a small amount of technical information when you use Spendly, such as your IP address, browser type, and timestamps of sign-ins. This information is used to keep your account secure and to diagnose problems with the service.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Spendly service.
  • Authenticate you and protect your account from unauthorized access.
  • Generate the category breakdowns, totals, and summaries that Spendly is built around.
  • Respond to your questions and support requests.
  • Send you important service notices, such as security alerts or policy changes.

We do not sell your data. We do not use the contents of your expense records to build advertising profiles, and we do not share your records with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers operated by trusted infrastructure providers. Access to your account is protected by password authentication, and sensitive fields such as passwords are hashed using modern algorithms — we and our staff cannot read your password in plain text.

While no system is perfectly secure, we take reasonable safeguards to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. If we ever become aware of a breach that affects your data, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

You may export or permanently delete your expense data at any time from your account settings. Deleting your account removes your records from our active systems, subject to any retention required for legal, accounting, or security purposes.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small number of third parties to operate. These include our hosting provider, our email delivery service (used for account-related messages such as password resets), and our analytics provider (used in aggregate, without identifying individual users).

When you sign in, we place a session cookie in your browser so the service can recognize you across requests. We do not use third-party advertising cookies, and we do not track you across other websites.

We share information with these providers only as needed to provide the Spendly service, and we require them to handle your data with a comparable level of protection.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You can review and update your account information at any time from your profile page. You can export a copy of your expense data, or delete your account and all associated records, from your account settings.

Depending on where you live, you may also have the right to request a copy of all personal data we hold about you, to correct inaccurate information, or to ask us to restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to the service, our practices, or applicable law. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

If the changes are material, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you — for example, by displaying a notice within the app or sending an email to the address associated with your account. Continued use of Spendly after the updated policy takes effect constitutes your acceptance of the changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app.
